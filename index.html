My synoptic story

I am Nasia, I am 15 years old, and I live in Greece. From the age of 10, I started programming. The very first thing I programmed was a robot guitar using Python. Until now, I have completed my dad’s web-page, and I developed a Python app that helps me learn new vocabulary. I am also 3D designer in my dad’s company. I create 3D models using SolidWorks, and I send the CAD files in factories for implementation.

I am always superhappy and excited to learn Astrophysics. Space is the sector I want to apply all the knowledge I will gain before and after university. For me, space is a server, and Earth is data. Space is an unlimited source of data; I think that is because space never stopped expanding.

Astrophysics paved me the way in a path called research. So far, I enjoy every single minute that I explore this path. At this time, I do research in multistrom cluster of satellites for cargo transfer in space.
